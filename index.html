Chocolate Covered Strawberries

first you need to boil a pot of water

then you put chocolate and shortening in a bowl and put it over the boiling water to melt

then stir the chocolate until fully melted

dip your freshly cleaned strawberry into the chocolate and let it cool

now enjoy your delicious chocolate covered strawberries !!

    milk
